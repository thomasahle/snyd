New Game with dice
Rules: Each player has a set of dice known only to them. Players take turns bidding on the minimum number of dice that the player believes are showing a given value. The player must bid a higher quantity of any particular face, or the same quantity of a higher face. You may challenge the previous bid, if you don't believe it, or you can't make any higher bids. 1s (âš€) are wild and count for every face (including 1). (ref. Wikipedia)
